Olive Health App

Project-Type

Voluntary, Part-Time

Role

Single work as a UX Designer applying a user centered process on my own. Doing research, defining potential users, user flows, creating wireframes & a final design.

Duration

6 Months

Skills Developed

UX Research, Information Architecture Wireframing, Usability Testing,UI Design & Prototyping

Goal

The goal was to create a health and wellbeing platform to manage medical information, create healthy routines, book doctor appointments as well as access physical and mental wellbeing trainings.

User Centered Design Process

Problem Statement

People struggle to focus on their health needs and conditions in this digital age. Potential users can easily get unmotivated or distracted if the effort is too much for the outcome. There needs to be an easy way for the user to manage their health intuitive and fast.

Research Questions

1.

How does the user tries to include healhty habits in their life?

2.

What featues or tasks would the user like to use on a health app?

3.

What are the motivations of the user to realize healhty habits?

4.

What are the users pain points on existing health apps on the market?

5.

Under which circumstances users would use the olive health app?
